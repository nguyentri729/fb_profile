What are Components?

Components are just copy and paste elements. Flexible, easy to use and incredibly fast loading to create gorgeous pages.

  • AccordionsKeep content focused and compressed.
  • ButtonsMultiple styles, colors, interact with users.
  • ChartsPowered by charts.js for power and features.
  • ChipsSmall interactive elements to show small information.
  • Content BoxesStyles, colors and gradients to house content
  • ColumnsOne, two or three columns. It's just one class.
  • Color PacksSimply add a class and you've got a new color.
  • DropdownsCompact links are awesome and easy to use.
  • DivdersSeparate content elements with beautiful dividers
  • GraphsPowered by charts.js for power and features
  • Google FontsThousands of fonts, just a copy paste away

  • Icon FontsFontAwesome 5 is here. Filled with awesome icons.
